STUDY ABROAD & VISA SERVICES

ASK HEMI provides a range of study, counselling and documentation services through our various designed packages online and offline.

All of our services guarantee our 100% effort towards customer satisfaction. The level of services varies with price and process involved during the journey.

Hold My Hand Service

Under Hold My Hand service, we will handle your visa and overseas study application. Our team of experts will be assigned to each client to handle and prepare their file precisely.In other words,we will be a holding hands together to cross it to your destination.

Inclusions: We will address all your queries as you navigate through the application process and provide clarity and guidance where needed. We will also review and scrutinize all your documents starting from offer letter compliance.

Correspondence: Most communication will be done over,direct phone calls through whatsspp and emails,. However, virtual meetings can be scheduled on request if you need to speak to a consultant.We also have a online customer service assistant.

Third Eye Service

The idea of Third Eye service is to maintain the transparency between us and our client through out their visa and admission procedure.These services are direct counselling and information package that involves our clients to participate in learning with us and prepare their own application to study abroad.Our team of expert counsellors provide wide range of information session through webinar and presentation and guide you in every step.Moreover,We will be your shadow during the entire journey starting from accepting a offer letter to visa grant.

Inclusions: We will provide a constant document and case file review service on your efforts and work and prepare it best before the submission of your application

Correspondence: Most communication will be done over direct phone calls through whatsapp and emails. The case file review will happen over an overscheduled zoom call.

Document Review Services

This service is specially designed for such aspiring clients, who are looking to pursue their study abroad and visa application on their own.However, need a quick review of their file before the final submission.

Our team of experts will review your entire file and generate a report which you can take as a reference to further submit.

NOTE: All of these services involves a cost which will allow them to access for 2 years.
Disclaimer: All our services are process-driven. Availing our services does not guarantee you a VISA, nor does it alter your eligibility or qualification for any other program.

ASK HEMI is a consulting and education service company operated by its team of qualified,trained experts and counsellors based in various locations around the globe. We, at ASK HEMI, are fully committed towards providing immense value while assisting you in your journey towards making your business/immigration/study dreams a reality.
